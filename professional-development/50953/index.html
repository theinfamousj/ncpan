Create A Unique Playground Using Hill Slides: Encore

  • Post author By Jennifer Levenbook
  • Post date February 28, 2022
  • 1 Comment on Create A Unique Playground Using Hill Slides: Encore

On Thursday, the 24th of March 2022, Playworld Inc. will be airing a CEU accredited webinar designed for those of us who are in the early childhood play space called “Create A Unique Playground Using Hill Slides“.

We here at your North Carolina Professional Association of Nannies think that the price (free) is right and the information is timely and so want to encourage all of our members and the Friends of NC PAN to attend the webinar.

To attend, one need only RSVP and then follow the instructions in the confirmation email (Zoom link).

However, for supporting members of the North Carolina Professional Association of Nannies, we will be hosting an encore viewing on Tuesday the 29th of March, starting at 7 pm, with a collaboration space for commentary and discussion. If you want to take advantage of this you will need the following technology: a web browser (preferably Chrome).

LINK TO JOIN the encore viewing

You will go to a webpage that looks an awful lot like Zoom in screen-share mode. There will be a chat function for typing chat, the capacity to turn on your camera and microphone, and a large space where the encore presentation of the webinar will be played, with sound. You are encouraged to eat your dinner while watching and collaborating with other NC PAN supporting member nannies. Spouses and housemates are welcome to look over your shoulder; they might learn something too!

We look forward to seeing everyone at one (or both) of the viewing sessions!
