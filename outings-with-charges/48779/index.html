Blueberry Picking (2015)

  • Post author By Jennifer Levenbook
  • Post date June 22, 2015
  • No Comments on Blueberry Picking (2015)

This is the first year that we are offering Blueberry Picking!

Learn how to RSVP, below.

Triangle + Triad

Contact: Denny Wilkerson Corsa, learn@ncpan.org

Come and pick blueberries with nannies and our charges! üôÇ

Open to NC PAN members, nannies who are not yet members of NC PAN, Friends of NC PAN, friends, moms, dads, etc.

RSVPs are MANDATORY. Members of NC PAN: RSVP through BigTent. Those who are not (yet) members: comment on this post as in the example.

In a Different Region?

Volunteer as host for your region’s Blueberry Picking on a day convenient to you during blueberry season! If you build it, they will come! Contact the NC PAN social chair via social@ncpan.org to get started. We will promote your event, add it to this post, help you gather RSVPs, and help you connect with nannies in your area.

All social events are volunteer hosted by member nannies!

Spread the Word!

Share this image to your favorite social media outlet, and add the details of your local region’s Blueberry Picking!
